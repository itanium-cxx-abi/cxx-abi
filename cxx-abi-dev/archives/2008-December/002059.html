So:

   DF <accumbits(opt)> <kind> <fractbits> <sat>
      <kind> = s for "signed short"
               t for "unsigned short"
               i for "signed"
               j for "unsigned"
               l for "long"
               m for "unsigned long"
      <sat> = s for _Sat, n for non-_Sat

This seems good to me, I've gone ahead and implemented it in GCC.
